Carel Education Associates Inc. is an Indiana-based charity working on promoting financial literacy and offering college-based skills to help with decisions in these areas. The mission is to provide complimentary education and financial literacy programs and leadership opportunities to teen youth, particularly the dis-advantaged, that will help them fulfill their potential as individuals, community members and contributing citizens.
