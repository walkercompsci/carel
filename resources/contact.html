Thinking of Sponsoring?

Annual Contribution

As you can see, our commitment to accelerate the CAI College Prep Program will require continued monetary gifts to our charity. We have no paid staff so all donated funds are applied directly to student from fund raisers (ie Yard Sale)

Here are examples:

$1 Will Pay for 1 writing tablet
$5 Will pay for One STEM Textbook or One lunch
$10 Will pay for a Volunteer T shirt
$195 Will purchase one refurbished laptop
$895 Will Sponsor one student in College Prep Program throughout their high school tenure

If you are interested in sponsoring, please contact us and we will reach out to you!

CAI has a working partnership with College Funding Solutions (CFS), a nationally ranked college prep organization based in Salem, OR. To obtain their advanced college prep services, their license to participate is REQUIRED. These license cost each student $895, but with your help and donations, we can make that FREE for each student and give them the skills and education needed to continue to a post-secondary school.
